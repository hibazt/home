Lemon

1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
Juice of 2 lemons
Zest of 1 lemon
2 cloves garlic, minced
Salt and pepper, to taste
Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter.

Add the minced garlic to the melted butter and saut√© for about 1-2 minutes, or until fragrant. Be careful not to let it brown.
Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the lemon juice and lemon zest. Mix well.
Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as needed.
Return the saucepan to low heat and simmer for a few minutes, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens slightly.
Once the sauce has thickened to your liking, remove it from the heat.
Pour the lemon butter sauce over your favorite cooked seafood, such as grilled shrimp or baked salmon, or use it as a drizzle for vegetables or pasta.
Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired.
This lemon butter sauce is a versatile and zesty condiment that adds a burst of flavor to a variety of dishes. Enjoy its bright and tangy goodness!
